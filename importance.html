1. Agricultural productivity can be increased by digitization and image analysis obtained from unmanned aerial vehicles and satellites.

2. Improving the collection, processing and dissemination of health data and information can enhance the diagnosis and treatment of patients, especially for those who live in rural and remote areas. Better data on climate and environmental conditions can also help governments better predict malaria, monitor disease outbreaks, and use medical resources more efficiently.

3. Artificial intelligence can be used to assess students' ability to achieve and help them be confident about major topics.

4. Artificial intelligence can help people with disabilities or special needs in multiple ways. Artificial intelligence is better for translation from text to voice and from voice to text, and in this way it can assist persons with visual or hearing disabilities in using information and communication technology.

5. Artificial intelligence really helps build smart, sustainable cities.

6. Analysis of data on climate change and AI-induced climate modeling predicts climate-related challenges and disasters .
